Internships

Workiva

For the last three years, I've been working for a company called Workiva. I've worked as a Software Engineer in Release Management, developing tools that make the process of developing code to deployment much faster. I've worked on a small team (4 developers), which has given me great experience in many areas of application development (UI, server, API's).

In my time at Workiva, I've worked on many projects. Because I've worked part-time during the school year and full-time over the summer, I've been able to work on and develop many different components of our application.

I've learned a lot from my internship at Workiva. On the technical side, I've learned a lot about database management, API development, UI design and development, object-oriented design, and ground-up application development. On the non-technical side, I've learned how to communicate effectively with members of my team. I've learned that one of the most important things to have among a team is conversation that's not related to what you're developing. I've noticed that team momentum significantly increases when each team member feels valued, and that they are known and cared about outside of the office. I've learned how to communicate with other teams and stakeholders as well.
